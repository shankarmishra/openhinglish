# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge
We want OpenHinglish to be a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of
background, experience level, identity, or language. We are building infrastructure for Indian
languages, and contributors from every region and skill level are valued.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful, patient, and constructive — especially with newcomers and non-native English speakers.
- Give and accept feedback graciously; critique ideas, not people.
- Assume good faith and help others learn.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory remarks of any kind.
- Personal or political attacks, or sustained disruption of discussions.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Scope
This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any official
communication channel — and when representing the project in public.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately to the maintainer:
**xshankarmishra@gmail.com**. Reports will be handled confidentially and reviewed promptly.

## Enforcement
The maintainer may warn, remove contributions from, or ban anyone who violates this code, at their
discretion, to keep the community safe and productive.

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant.
